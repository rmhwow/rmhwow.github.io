About Me

My name is Morgan O'Leary. I'm currently a junior at Lawrence University studying Spanish and German with a government minor. This year I've been traveling all over cultivating new skills. I studied abroad in Granda, Spain and I also spent the spring semester working for a small non-profit in Washington D.C. I found myself wanting to learn more about computer technology and my parents stumbled across Dev Bootcamp in the Wall Street Journal. With some encouragement, I decided to apply and was overjoyed to be accepted. I can't wait to see what the future holds in my DBC career and beyond. Please, feel free to contact me!
